What Is This Trip?

Friends Riding Together
Through Baja

We're planning a group ride to Baja California in advance of the BMW Motorcycle Club of Northern California's annual Death Valley Trip.

This is not a club ride but organized to help club members who want to experience Baja on a motorcycle with some of the planning and logistics handled.

It's not a commercial group tour, just your fellow riders helping organize it for their friends.

  • check_circle Coordinated nightly stops at established locations
  • check_circle Mix of organized group meals and free time
  • check_circle Ride at your own pace, meet up at day's end
