## SECTION 1: ACKNOWLEDGMENT OF RISKS

I, the undersigned participant, understand and acknowledge that participation in motorcycle touring activities organized by NorCal Moto Adventure ("Organizer") involves inherent and significant risks that cannot be eliminated regardless of the care taken to avoid them.

### I understand that these risks include, but are not limited to:

**Motorcycle-Related Risks:**
- Collision with other vehicles, objects, or pedestrians
- Loss of control of the motorcycle
- Mechanical failure or breakdown
- Tire blowouts or punctures
- Road hazards including potholes, gravel, sand, oil, debris, and uneven surfaces
- Adverse weather conditions including rain, wind, heat, cold, and reduced visibility
- Fatigue from extended riding
- Equipment failure or malfunction

**Environmental and Location Risks:**
- Remote locations with limited access to emergency services
- Unmarked or poorly maintained roads
- Wildlife on or near roadways
- Extreme temperatures and sun exposure
- Altitude-related illness
- Limited access to food, water, fuel, or shelter
- Natural disasters including earthquakes, floods, and wildfires

**International Travel Risks (for tours in Mexico or other countries):**
- Different traffic laws and driving customs
- Language barriers affecting communication
- Limited or different quality of medical care
- Political instability or civil unrest
- Currency and banking issues
- Customs and immigration complications
- Different emergency response systems

**Medical Risks:**
- Serious physical injury including broken bones, lacerations, burns, and head injuries
- Permanent disability including paralysis
- Traumatic brain injury
- Internal injuries
- Heat stroke, dehydration, or hypothermia
- Allergic reactions
- Pre-existing medical conditions aggravated by activity
- **Death**

**Other Risks:**
- Actions or negligence of other participants
- Actions or negligence of third parties including other motorists
- Theft or loss of personal property
- Inadequate or delayed emergency response
- Communication failures in remote areas
