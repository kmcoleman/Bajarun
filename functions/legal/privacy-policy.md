# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025

NorCal Moto Adventure ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and website.

## Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

- Personal information (name, email address, phone number)
- Emergency contact information
- Motorcycle and riding experience details
- Profile photos and uploaded documents
- Accommodation and tour preferences
- Payment information (processed securely through Stripe)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Organize and manage motorcycle tours
- Communicate tour details, updates, and alerts
- Process registrations and payments
- Coordinate accommodations and logistics
- Contact emergency contacts if needed during tours
- Improve our services and user experience

## Information Sharing

We share your information with other tour participants (name, photo, city) to facilitate group coordination. Emergency contact information is only accessed in case of emergency. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

## Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information using Firebase Authentication and Firestore with strict security rules. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

## Your Rights

You may access, update, or delete your personal information by contacting us at support@futurepathdevelopment.com. You may also request a copy of your data.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" above.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@futurepathdevelopment.com.
